Fig Smoothie

Description

The sticky texture of figs turns this smoothie into a thick and delicious drink for fig lovers (or anyone who needs to use this perishable fruit fast)!

Ingredients

  • 2 frozen bananas, peeled and chopped
  • 6 fresh figs, halved
  • ¾ cup milk
  • ¾ cup orange juice

Steps

  1. Place the bananas, figs, milk, and orange juice into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth. Pour into glasses to serve.
